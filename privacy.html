Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2024

Home Architecture ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Email Address: For account creation and communication
  • Photos: Room photos you upload for AI processing
  • Purchase History: Credits purchased through in-app purchases
  • Device Information: For app functionality and bug fixes

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide AI interior design services
  • Manage your account and credits
  • Send important service updates
  • Improve the app experience

3. Photo Privacy

Important: Your uploaded photos are processed temporarily for AI generation and are deleted immediately after processing. We never use your photos to train AI models or share them with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with essential service providers:

  • Supabase: Secure database storage
  • Replicate: AI processing (photos deleted after generation)
  • Apple/Google: Payment processing

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent

6. Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all transmissions
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Uploaded photos: Deleted immediately after AI processing
  • AI results: Kept until you delete them
  • Transaction records: 7 years (legal requirement)

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions:

Email: privacy@tavadi.net

Support: support@tavadi.net
